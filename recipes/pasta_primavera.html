Pasta Primavera

Description

Pasta primavera is a delicious and vibrant dish that celebrates the flavors of spring. It typically features a medley of fresh vegetables such as bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and broccoli, tossed with pasta and a light sauce. This colorful and flavorful pasta dish is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (such as spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • Assorted vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, broccoli, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (such as basil, parsley, or thyme)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using), and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped vegetables to the skillet and cook until tender-crisp, stirring occasionally.
  4. Season the vegetables with salt, pepper, and any desired fresh herbs.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta with the vegetable mixture until well combined.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy!
